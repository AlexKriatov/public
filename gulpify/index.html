Features

Designed for consistency, not clutter

Adaptive hydration goals

  • Goal estimation that adjusts to your pace and reminders.
  • Supports custom drink types and volumes.
  • Offline-first and instant sync when online.

Insightful tracking

  • Daily and weekly charts to spot patterns.
  • Calendar history for quick backfill and edits.
  • Lightweight streaks that encourage, not nag.

Calm experience

  • No accounts, no ads—your data stays on device.
  • Accessible UI with thoughtful motion and haptics.
  • Built with Flutter for a smooth native feel.
